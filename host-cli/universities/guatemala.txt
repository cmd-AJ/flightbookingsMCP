Top Three Universities in Guatemala

1. Universidad de San Carlos de Guatemala (USAC)
   - Founded in 1676
   - The oldest and largest public university in Guatemala
   - Located in Guatemala City

2. Universidad Rafael Landívar (URL)
   - Founded in 1961
   - Private Jesuit university
   - Known for its high academic standards and research programs

3. Universidad Francisco Marroquín (UFM)
   - Founded in 1971
   - Private university focused on free market economics
   - Recognized for its business and economics programs
